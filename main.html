Syrniki (Cottage cheese pancakes)


Syrniki are a deliciously tasty and familiar home-style treat.

They are served with honey, condensed milk, jam, or powdered sugar.



Ingredients:

  • Cottage cheese (dry) - 250-300g;
  • Egg - 1 pc;
  • Wheat flour - 2-3 tbsp. tablespoons;
  • Sugar - 1 tbsp. spoon;
  • Salt - a pinch;
  • Vanillin (or vanilla sugar) - a pinch;
  • Refined vegetable oil - 2 tbsp (for frying).


Preparation:

  1. Mix cottage cheese and eggs in a blender until smooth.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and flour. Add the sugar and vanilla sugar last, because it provokes the separation of moisture from the cottage cheese. Mix the dough well. Form into 9-10 balls.
  3. Cover the surface with flour and use your hands to form cheesecakes (shaped like pucks).
  4. Fry in vegetable oil until golden brown 3-4 minutes on each side over moderate heat. You can cover with a lid so that the syrniki are well baked inside.
  5. Put the finished syrniki on a paper towel to absorb excess fat. Serve the syrniki with your favorite sauces, sour cream, jam, honey, etc.

Bon Appetit!
